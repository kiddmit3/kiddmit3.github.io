My Career

Thales Digital Identity and Security (6 Years)

I’ve built secure government web applications, delivering full-stack solutions that meet strict security and compliance standards. I led the development of platforms such as WebID and Web Archive, and contributed to several mission-critical systems. My work involved modernizing legacy technology, customizing deployments to client needs, and supporting high-stakes implementations both remotely and on-site. Later, I transitioned into R&D, where I helped transform projects into scalable, productized solutions deployed globally as part of Thales’s Evidence & Investigation Suite.

Infinite Promotions (1 Year)

I built scalable applications under tight deadlines and evolving business needs. I developed a platform for real estate agents to automate social media management, including tools for auto-posting listings and articles, generating e-flyers and videos, and managing leads. My contributions spanned from creating an interactive client portal and admin dashboard to designing the product’s UI, logo, and marketing page. I also implemented a large-scale web scraper that collected over 1 million real estate leads.

Beta Boulders App (2 Years)

I designed and developed an application for rock climbers that allowed users to scan climb tags, upload and stream video solutions, view annotated images, and track progress. The app featured real-time competition scoreboards via WebSockets, dynamic QR code scanning, and gym-side tools for managing climbs and member engagement. Built with Next.js, Express.js, and MongoDB and with the help of Apache Cordova, it was deployed to Google Play and the App Store. Through this experience, I gained firsthand insight into how marketing, product strategy, and business growrth is just as critical as engineering, and later collaborated with KAYA to transition core features after retiring the app.
