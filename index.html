About Me

I started making webpages with HTML2 and CSS 15 years ago, publishing my first blog using Angelfire. I was so fascinated with the technology that I spent countless nights learning and building (and vise versa). To this day, I never lost track of this passion. Technology is a huge part of my life. I am a full-stack developer, with JavaScript as my language of choice. My experience includes working with modern frameworks such as ReactJS in creating micro-services employing NodeJS, ExpressJS, and mySQL/MongoDB over cloud.
