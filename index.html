A Little About Me

I am an end to end product developer that understands that the customer/user is the single most valuable asset in the product stream and that working together as a team and doing your part as an individual is what accomplishes goals and help reach milestones.

I am User Focused

I enjoy making products that are both easy to use and easy on the eyes. I picture how I would use the applications I am building and imagine what features are needed. To that end, I work across the entire system architecture, from backend to front end, to design, develop, and support high quality and scalable products that allows for a more unified user experience matched with high performance, security, quality, and stability.

Easy to work with
